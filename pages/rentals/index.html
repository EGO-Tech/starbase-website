Ship Rentals/Test Drive

Want to try out one of the ships without printing it on LIVE or going to the PTU? You can by booking a test drive. Ships will be loaned out to you for 24 hours for FREE, safe zone only.

Ships are limited so availability may vary. Ships may also not be the current version.

To book a ship, please join the EGOTech Discord server.
