Series: Hexxer

Hexxer

v1.1.0 Asteroid Capacity 6 x T7 Loading Speed Moderate

The Hexxer is a ship for hauling small asteroids, designed to be used in the safe zone. It uses six tractor beams to pull asteroids into six compartments of cargo lock beams in a hex pattern. It has load automation to approach and load asteroids in the right order to balance the ship as much as possible as well as a material scanner that gives estimated credits for selling the asteroids.

Hexxer Trio

v1.1.0 Asteroid Capacity 3 x T10 Loading Speed Fast

The Hexxer Trio is the enlarged version of the Hexxer for hauling the maximum three T10 asteroids, designed to be used in the safe zone. It has load automation to approach and load the asteroid as well as a material scanner that gives estimated credits for selling the asteroids.
